So, before we build —
one small, old thing.
On the strip of paper — two lines, no name
One Something you are nurturing
in your life — or would like to.
Two How it feels to tend it.
then
Fold it.
Let it fall into the bowl.
then
Take one.
if it is your own, return it, and draw again
then
A few voices will read
the strip in their hands.
someone else's words, carried in your voice

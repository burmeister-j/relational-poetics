What do we
care for?
On the strip of paper — two lines, no name
One Something you are nurturing
in your life — or would like to.
Two How it feels to tend it.
then
Fold your two lines.
Let them fall from your hands.
then
Take one.
Hold what someone is letting go.
if it is your own, return it, and draw again
then
Lend your voice
to that care.
someone else's words, carried in the room
